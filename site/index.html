How It Works

From hunt to glory in 8 easy steps

1
🥚 Find eggs of YOUR color
Each player hunts for their assigned egg color only
2
✨ Grab any special two-tone eggs you spot
Two-tone pink/purple eggs are fair game for everyone
3
📯 Return when the whistle blows
15-minute hunt — when you hear it, head back!
4
🤝 Trading phase — swap task eggs
5 minutes to negotiate blind trades with other players
5
💫 Reveal power-ups — game-changers applied!
Score multipliers, bonus Skittles, and wild twists
6
🔢 Count your Skittles
Crack open your eggs and tally up the loot
7
🎭 Open task eggs — perform hilarious challenges!
Complete tasks for bonus Skittles (prepare to be embarrassed)
8
👑 Winner crowned!
Most Skittles wins — glory and prizes await

The Rules

Read them. Know them. No excuses.

⏰
15-Minute Time Limit
The hunt clock is strict. Make every second count!
🌎
Stay Within Park Boundaries
If you can't see the park, you've gone too far.
🎨
YOUR Color + Special Eggs Only
Find your assigned color eggs and any two-tone pink/purple specials.
🚫
No Picking Up Other Players' Eggs
See a color that isn't yours? Walk on by.
🥷
No Hiding Eggs — No Sabotage!
If you find it, take it or leave it. No re-hiding!
📣
Return Immediately at the Whistle
When you hear the signal, head back to base ASAP.
🥚
Special Egg Difficulty Guide
All pink = easy task. Top pink = medium. Bottom pink = hard. Choose wisely during trades!
