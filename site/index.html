The Hop-by-Hop Guide

From first hop to champion bunny in 8 springy steps!

1
🥚 Hop to it — find YOUR eggs!
Every bunny has their own color — sniff out only yours!
2
✨ Spot a two-tone egg? Snatch it up!
Two-tone eggs aren't assigned to anyone — any player can grab them!
3
📯 Hop back when the hunting horn sounds!
The Great Hunt lasts 15 hops of the clock — then scurry home!
4
🤝 The Bunny Bazaar — trade your task eggs!
5 minutes to wheel and deal with fellow bunnies (no peeking!)
5
💫 Spring surprises — power-ups unleashed!
Score multipliers, bonus Skittles, and wild springtime twists!
6
🔢 Crack 'em open — count your rainbow loot!
Smash those shells and tally up your Skittles treasure!
7
🎭 Task egg time — silly spring challenges!
Complete tasks for bonus Skittles (prepare for some egg-streme embarrassment!)
8
👑 The champion bunny is crowned!
Most Skittles wins — eternal egg-hunting glory awaits!

The Special Eggs Guide

Two-tone eggs hold secret tasks — and every bunny can grab them!

🌱 Easy Peasy Task
All pink, both halves — a gentle challenge even the smallest bunny can handle!
🌸 Medium Mischief Task
Top half pink, bottom half white — a bit more hop in your step required!
🔥 Hard-Boiled Challenge
Top half pink, bottom half purple — only the bravest bunnies dare! Big Skittles rewards!
🐰 Remember: Two-tone eggs are free for ALL hunters — no assigned color needed! Grab every one you see!

The Bunny Code

Every good bunny knows the rules of the meadow!

⏰
The Great Hunt Lasts 15 Hops of the Clock!
When the clock runs out, even the speediest bunny has to stop hopping!
🌎
Don't Wander Beyond the Bunny Trail!
If you can't see the meadow, you've hopped too far, little bunny!
🎨
Your Eggs + Sparkly Specials Only!
Hop after your own color eggs and any two-tone treasures you spot!
🚫
Every Bunny for Themselves!
No snatching eggs from another hunter's trail, and no whispering where the eggs are hiding — that's fowl play! 🐔
🥷
No Egg-scape Artists Allowed!
Found an egg? Take it or leave it — no sneaky re-hiding or bunny business!
📣
Hop Back to the Nest When the Horn Sounds!
When the hunting horn blows, scurry back to base like a bunny who heard a fox!
🥚
Two-Tone Eggs = Mystery Tasks!
Check the Special Eggs Guide above to decode which colors mean easy, medium, or hard — trade wisely at the Bunny Bazaar!
