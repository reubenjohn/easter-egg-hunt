**Shopping tips for eggs:**
- Bulk packs of 48-count single-color eggs are cheapest (Dollar Tree, Walmart, Amazon)
- For two-tone special eggs: buy extra pink and purple eggs and swap halves to create the combos
- If exact colors aren't available, pick the closest match and note any color substitutions
- Buy early — egg supplies sell out fast near Easter

## Where to Buy

| Store | Best For |
|-------|----------|
| **Dollar Tree** | Plastic eggs (bulk), baskets, bags, bunny ears, tablecloth |
| **Walmart / Target** | Skittles, chocolate bunny, M&M carrots, eggs if Dollar Tree is out |
| **Amazon** | Bulk single-color eggs (if stores don't have exact colors), bunny ears multipacks |
| **Walgreens / CVS** | Last-minute candy, prizes, wet wipes |

**Buy eggs ASAP** — they sell out 1-2 weeks before Easter. Candy and prizes can wait until closer to the date.
